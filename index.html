My Services

Data Collection and cleaning

Data analysts are responsible for gathering and preparing data for analysis. This includes identifying relevant sources of data, collecting and importing data into a usable format, and cleaning and preprocessing the data to ensure it is accurate and consistent.

Learn more

Data Analysis and modelling

Once data has been collected and cleaned, data analysts use statistical and computational methods to analyze the data and identify patterns and trends. This may include developing predictive models, conducting statistical tests, and creating visualizations to help communicate the results of the analysis.

Learn more

Data communication and reporting

Data analysts are responsible for communicating the results of their analysis to stakeholders.This includes creating reports,presentations,and visualizations that clearly and effectively communicate the key findings and insights from the data. Additionally, data analysts may be involved in creating dashboards and other interactive tools to help stakeholders explore and understand the data.
